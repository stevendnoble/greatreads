Welcome to Word Wizzərd!

Word Wizzərd is a collection of word games using passages from classic literature. Be sure to enter your name at the top right to play and keep track of your scores.

Games Available:

Grammaring

Players provide words for given parts of speech. The words are then inserted into the passage. The story can be read aloud using several different voices.


Unscrambling

Players must unscramble words using context clues. The game is timed, giving players a bonus for unscrambling all the words quickly.


Proofreading

Players are tasked to find the misspelled words in a story. For hints, the story can be read aloud allowing the user to listen for mistakes.
